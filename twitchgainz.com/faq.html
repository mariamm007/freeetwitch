What is an Offer?

An Offer is what you use to entice other users to engage with your channel. For example, you can create a Follow Offer to reward a user in Gems for following your channel, or you can create a Stream Offer to reward a user in Gems for viewing your live stream for a set amount of time.

I created a Follow Offer but I'm not getting followers

First, please keep in mind that all of the followers you receive from Free Twitch Views are REAL users, not bots like some of the other growth services out there. With that said, you won't receive an instant and massive amount of viewers all at once; instead, you will see a gradual and more natural influx of viewers. This should actually be seen as a pro rather than a con because Twitch will suspend accounts with bot-like activity.

 

Second, the Offer Feed is sorted by the amount of Gems being offered. If you offer 80 Gems per follow and another user offers 100, their Offer will appear above yours. If you find that your Offer is shown too low on the Offer Feed, try offering a higher amount of Gems.

 

Lastly, make sure that your Offer is live by looking at the My Offers page. You can also see your Offer's completion percentage on that page as well.
